What to Expect Week by Week
Week 1–2
You'll feel sore. That's good.
Your muscles are waking up. Scale may not move yet — that's normal. Focus on showing up, not results.
Week 3
Soreness fades. Routine locks in.
You'll stop dreading the gym and start expecting it. This is where most people quit — don't.
Week 4
First visible change.
Waistline starts to tighten slightly. Shirts may fit a little different. Energy noticeably higher.
Week 5
Phase 2 kicks in. Add weight.
Increase every lift by 5 lbs. You're stronger than you were — prove it. Belly fat visibly reducing.
Week 6
Other people start to notice.
You'll hear "you look different" before you see it yourself. Shoulder and arm definition emerges.
Week 7
Momentum is real.
Diet feels automatic. Gym is a non-negotiable. Body composition is measurably different.
Week 8
Day 60. Take the photo.
You should be down 5–8 lbs of fat and noticeably more muscular. The real goal: you built a habit that lasts beyond 60 days.

Make This Stick
  • 📸 Take a photo today. Front and side. You'll want this at day 60 — trust the process.
  • ⚖️ Weigh yourself once a week, same morning, same time. Daily weigh-ins lie — water weight fluctuates 3–5 lbs day to day.
  • 💧 Drink a gallon of water a day. Seriously. Cuts appetite, helps you lift more, flushes belly bloat.
  • 😴 Sleep 7–8 hours. Muscle is built while you sleep, not while you lift. Poor sleep tanks your progress more than a missed workout.
  • 🥩 Hit your protein every single day. You can miss cardio. You can miss a workout. Never miss your protein target — it's the #1 factor in keeping muscle.
  • 🍺 Beer on weekends, not weekdays. Alcohol spikes cortisol which stores belly fat. Keep it to Fri/Sat and you're fine.
  • 🍟 One cheat meal a week is fine. One cheat day will undo progress. Eat the burger — skip the whole Saturday binge.
  • 🏋️ Progressive overload is the secret. Add 5 lbs to every lift every 1–2 weeks. That's it. That's the whole muscle-building formula.
  • ⏰ Go at the same time every day. Make it a calendar event. The gym you almost skip always feels best after.
  • 🎵 Build a hype playlist. Sounds dumb, works immediately. 15% more output on average with music you're pumped about.
